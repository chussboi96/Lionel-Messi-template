I am Lionel Messi

Professional Footballer

Leo Messi (born 24 June 1987), is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward. Widely regarded as the greatest players of all time, Messi has won a record seven Ballon d'Or awards and a record six European Golden Shoes, and in 2020 he was named to the Ballon d'Or Dream Team. Until leaving the club in 2021, he had spent his entire professional career with Barcelona, where he won a club-record 34 trophies, including ten La Liga titles, seven Copa del Rey titles and the UEFA Champions League four times. A prolific goalscorer and creative playmaker, Messi has scored over 800 senior career goals for club and country.
